Alfredo Sauce

Description:

This delectable dish is full of butter, cheese, and garlic... what's not to love? Deceptively simple and dangerously artery clogging, this heart attack on a plate is sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Angel Hair Pasta
  • 1 Stick Unsalted Butter
  • 5-20 Cloves Diced Garlic (Depending on Taste)
  • 8 Oz Cream Cheese
  • 5 Oz Grated Parmesean Cheese
  • 1 Pint Half and Half
  • 1/8 Cup Milk
  • Italian Seasoning to Taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare noodles according to directions on box.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt butter and add in diced garlic. Stir 1-2 minutes or until garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add in cream cheese. Continue to stir and break up cream cheese until fully incorporated, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add in half and half, milk, and parmesean cheese.
  5. Whisk continuously until parmesean is melted. If the sauce is too thick, add another 1/8 cup milk.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and italian seasoning to taste.
  7. Enjoy!
